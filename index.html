GeForce Now

Nvidia GeForce Now is a cloud-streaming service that gives players the chance to stream certain games to their devices via a strong internet connection, in the same vein as services like Google Stadia and Xbox Game Pass. By using the servers via the cloud, it doesn’t matter what kind of hardware you’re using, as long as you can access the required app. This means you can play high-end PC games on your phone, tablet or laptop.
